<b>SmartScan</b> is an innovative app powered by a <b>CLIP model</b> that 
automatically organizes your images by <b>content similarity</b> and enables 
<b>text-based search</b>, making gallery management effortless.

<b>Key Features</b>

<b>Automated Image Organisation</b>  
<ul>
<li><b>Smart Scans:</b> Schedule regular scans automatically.</li>
<li><b>Auto-Categorisation Test:</b> Upload a test image to verify categorisation.</li>
</ul>

<b>Text-Based Image Search</b>  
<ul>
<li><b>Gallery Access:</b> Open search results directly in the gallery.</li>
<li><b>Result Display:</b> Show the top result plus up to 20 similar images.</li>
<li><b>Weekly Updates:</b> Automatically refresh the image index for new images.</li>
</ul>
