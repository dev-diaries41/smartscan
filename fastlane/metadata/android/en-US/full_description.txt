Search images and videos offline using text or images, and automatically organize your images with on-device AI.

<b>Key Features</b>

<b>Search Images and Videos</b>
<ul>
<li><b>Search Options:</b> Search using text or images.</li>
<li><b>Folder Selection:</b> Choose which image and video folders are allowed to be indexed.</li>
<li><b>Gallery Access:</b> Open search results directly in the gallery.</li>
<li><b>Weekly Updates:</b> Automatically refresh image and video indexes for new content.</li>
</ul>

<b>Automated Image Organisation</b>
<ul>
<li><b>Auto-Organisation:</b> Schedule regular scans automatically to organise images based on content similarity.</li>
<li><b>Auto-Organisation Test:</b> Upload a test image to verify categorisation.</li>
<li><b>Undo Scans:</b> Revert the most recent scan if needed.</li>
</ul>

<b>Fully On-Device</b>
All processing including both image organisation and text-based search is handled entirely on-device, ensuring privacy, speed, and offline functionality.
